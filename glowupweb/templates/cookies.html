Cookies Policy

At Glow Up, we use cookies to enhance your experience on our website and to analyze traffic on our services.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember your preferences and improve the site's functionality.

Types of cookies we use

  • Necessary cookies: Essential for the proper functioning of the website.
  • Performance cookies: Help analyze user behavior to improve website performance.
  • Functionality cookies: Allow us to remember user preferences to offer a personalized experience.
  • Advertising cookies: Used to display personalized ads and manage advertising campaigns.

How to manage cookies

If you wish, you can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our site.

More information

For more details on how we manage personal information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.


If you have any questions or concerns about our cookie policy, feel free to contact us.
